The moon

A Natural Satellite


The Moon is the only natueral satellite of the earth, and the fifth largest satellite in the
Solar System. It is the largest natural satellite of a planet in the Solar System relative
to the size of its primary, having a quarter the diameter of the Earth but only 1/81 its'
mass.
